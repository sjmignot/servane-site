The 30th edition of the famous international contemporary craft exhibition was juried by Bruce W. Pepich of the Racine Art Museum (RAM). It showcases works in basketry, ceramics, decorative fiber, furniture, glass, jewelry, leather, metal, mixed media, paper, wearable art, wood and 3-D printing.
