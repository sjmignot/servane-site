The Decatur Arts Alliance presents the thirteenth edition of the juried exhibition of The Book as Art, which will be installed at the Decatur Branch of the DeKalb County Public Library, home to the Georgia Center for the Book. The Book as Art series seeks to showcase beautifully made volumes, sculptural and conceptual interpretations, and one of a kind approaches to the book format.
