An international exhibition of artists' books that utilize a form of traditional hand printed media as primary element. The show is juried by Katherine Crowe, Special Collections Curator, University of Denver Libraries and Alicia Bailey, Director of Abecedarian Artists’ Books. It is part of Colorado’s  Mo’Print Celebration 2026 (a month of printmaking).
