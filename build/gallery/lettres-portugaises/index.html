Lettres Portugaises

Binding

Pianel (invented by Ben Elbel).

details

  • author: Marianna Alcoforado
  • illustrator: Modigliani
  • press: Ed. Mermod
  • year: 1952

Dimensions

18 x 12 x 1.5 cm

Materials

washi, elephant hide and Cave paper, archival digital printing, sensuede, brass rods.

An elegant and simple binding for a lovely edition of a book that was first published anonymously in Paris in 1669.
