Correspondances

Binding

folding and encaustic

Dimensions

53 x 53 x 41 cm

Edition

1 of 1

Materials

archival digital printing, beeswax

Inspired by long walks among the trees of Palo Alto and the Stanford campus and the poetry of Charles Baudelaire.

It is also a project I would like to make on a much larger scale as a public art installation so that we could walk around the tree/living pillars, marvel at their beauty and be transported, body and soul!
